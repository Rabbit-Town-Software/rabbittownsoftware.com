About laceypresley.com

laceypresley.com is a minimalist, retro-styled portfolio website for artist Lacey Presley, featuring a curated selection of works across traditional, digital, and mixed media formats. The design evokes early web aesthetics while staying clean and responsive. Built completely with HTML and CSS, this site is intentionally lightweight, fast-loading, and easy to maintain.
