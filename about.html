About Rabbit Town Software

Rabbit Town Software is an independent, developer-run studio dedicated to building open-source software crafted with a user-first mentality and a commitment to digital ethics.

Every project we release is licensed under the GPL-3.0, meaning it’s entirely free to use, study, share, and modify. We don’t track users, collect data, or sell information—our work is built on a foundation of privacy, transparency, and digital freedom.
