A minimalist QR scanner built for speed and privacy. No ads, no clutter, just results.

About PlainQR

PlainQR is a fast, no-nonsense QR code scanner for Android. It launches instantly, scans quickly using Google ML Kit, and never collects your data. It’s the ideal tool when you just want to scan and go—without ads or distractions.

Features

  • Ultra-fast QR code recognition
  • Ad-free, tracking-free experience
  • One-tap visit or dismiss interface
  • Auto-resume scanning when returning to app
  • Clean interface, built with Jetpack Compose
